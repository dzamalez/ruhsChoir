FAQs

What classes are offered in choir?

Beginning Choir, Chorale (Intermediate Mixed Ensemble), Advanced Women's Ensemble, and Choraliers (Advanced Mixed Ensemble) are all offered at RUHS. For more information about these groups, click here.

Is there a fee for choir? How much is it?

The audition choirs (Chorale, Advanced Women's Ensemble, and Choraliers) are all required to pay the $50 activity fee through the finance office. Students must also fill out and submit an emergency card specifically for choir. See above for an instructional document detailing how to complete the online registration as well as the link to the online emergency card registration website.

What other costs are there to be in choir?

Other costs for choir include attire rental/purchase. Chorale and Advanced Women females rent their dresses. Choralier women purchase their tops and skirts. Chorale and Choralier men purchase their attire specified in the handbook. Students will usually be asked to pay a cleaning fee of around $20. Speak with Ms. Boosamra-Ball if you have questions.

What is Concert Choir? Do I have to be in that?

You can find the description of Concert Choir here. Only those students in Chorale, Advanced Women, and Choraliers are in this choir.
