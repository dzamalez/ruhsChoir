Volunteering

Below is a list of the volunteering opportunities for the 2021-22 school year. Please fill out the form to the right and select a committee from the drop-down menu. You can sign up for more than one opportunity and each opportunity can never have too many volunteers! If you would like to be the committee chair for a particular activity, please specify. Committee chairs will be responsible for organizing the other volunteers. I will update the list of volunteers on the right as submissions come in! ALL DATES TBA.

  • Car Washes - Volunteers will help supervise one or more car washes throughout the year.
