Welcome to the 2021-2022 school year of the Rincon/University High Choral Program!

This website was designed to assist you and your family to stay as informed and up-to-date with this year’s choir activities as possible. By using this website, it is our hope to keep you apprised of all performances, information, and any changes that may occur, throughout the school year. RUHS Choirs are one of the finest choral music programs in the State of Arizona. During the year, regardless of the choir, members will experience many different types of music, learn more in the way of music theory, music history, and make some of the strongest friendships of their lives. The goal will be to learn, as a group, to share ideas, goals, and our love for music.
